Principal Message


Principal

BS College for Girl's breathes life into contemporary learning, motivates girls to strive to accomplish their goals and celebrates their many talents. The College provides a high quality academic education with extensive co-curricular opportunities, and aims to introduce young women a strong sense of self-esteem and confidence. Our commitment is to provide a safe and intellectually challenging environment that will empower students to become innovative thinkers, creative problem solvers and inspired learners prepared to flourish in the twenty-first century. With experienced and dedicated teachers and excellent infrastructure, our college helps students to realize their goals in life. I encourage all students to take advantage of the opportunities provided by the college and involve themselves in all the extra-curricular activities that are offered. Together we strive to welcome, to believe, to respect, to learn and to achieve our full potential.

Prof. Anum Safdar

M.Phil Computer Science
