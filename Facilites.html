Computer Lab

Computer lab is ideal for computer-training needs including software releases, user-group meetings, and new application training. Our lab includes student Laptops and one instructor Laptop, Wired and wireless Internet access and printer are included. Some highlights and features of our computer lab include: Air conditioned lab with Electronic rostrum and Multimedia projector, Comfortable, ergonomic seating In-house expertise to ensure flawless presentations and interactivity and Alternate days for Boys as well as Girls.

Biology Lab

The automated Biology Labs are capable of providing the controlled environment to carry out sensitive experiments. These gleaming and spacious Labs can serve dozens of students simultaneously.

Physics Lab

Our spacious Physics Labs are operational with the modern lab equipment’s. Along with these up-to-date equipment’s, the lab incharges can supervise up to 30 students at a time to carry their practical work.

Chemistry Lab

Sunny and bright Chemistry Labs are free of the pinching smells of acids and chemicals which traditionally cause suffocation as you enter an outdated Chemistry lab. At a time, a pack of 30 students can work there with ease.

Library

The college library presents the updates, the expensive local and foreign books on economics, trade, medical and engineering fields. Library plays a crucial role in every academic system. It is a reflection of the intellectual health of an institution and is the heart beat of a buzzing civilization. Not only does it consolidate class-room environment, but also gives students a golden opportunity to delve deep into a subject. It supports knowledge-seekers to attain the zenith of their an uncharted triumph.
