Rules & Regulations


Code of Conduct

It is binding upon every student of the college to follow the following rules and regulations:

  • Every student has to demonstrate tolerance, patience, and politeness in her dealing with teachers and fellow students;
  • Every student should respect the college teachers and the college administration and abide by the rules and regulations introduced from time to time
  • Every student should pledge to keep her mind and body clean and must demonstrate good manners and should develop the spirit to help and serve her fellow students;
  • Every student must take care of the public and college property and persuade others to do so;
  • Every student, for any personal concern, has to directly contact the concerned Incharge. Improper interference in the academic, administrative, sporting, social or other activities is strictly prohibited;
  • Every student must come to the college in the college prescribed uniform;
  • Every student must provide correct information to the college administration, whenever required;
  • Every student of the College is required to be punctual in the classes. Any student remaining absent from classes without proper permission shall be suspended/ struck off from the College Roll unless the cause of absence is explained to the satisfaction of the Principal as per College Rules
  • No student is allowed to leave the class without the permission of her teacher or until the class is over.
  • Latecomers in the class may be marked absent. A fine will be imposed for absence without proper permission as per College Rules
  • Absentees for three consecutive working days without any notice or valid reason shall be considered for expulsion.
  • Students must keep with them the College identity Card during their stay in the College premises and during College functions.
  • Unfair means in quizzes, assignments, exams will result in punishment with heavy fine or suspension.
  • Students are not allowed to use or keep cellular phones/cameras except allowed by the college administration under special circumstances. Mobiles and cameras confiscated shall only be returned if the Disciplinary Committee permits
  • The college will not be responsible for any loss, theft of personal belongings.
  • Any student misbehaving in class or on campus will be reported to the Principal by the teacher. The concerned authority will take the necessary action deemed appropriate.
  • The College authorities can revise dues without any prior notice. This revision will be applicable to all students enrolled at that time or in future.
ACCOUNTABLE IRREGULARITIES
Students are required to observe and maintain a reasonable standard of conduct. Any act of misconduct committed by a student inside or outside the campus shall mean the violation of the college discipline resulting in penalties of different intensities including expulsion, rustication or struck-off from the college.
Committing any or more of the following will be considered as misconduct and violation of college discipline:
  • Disruption of, or improper interference with, the academic, administrative, sporting, social or other activities of the college, including any attempt to prevent any staff member, student or visitor of the college from carrying on his or her work;
  • Fraud, deceit, deception, dishonesty or forgery, tampering with or misuse of the college documents or records, identification cards etc.;
  • An action which causes or is likely to cause injury or impair safety on college premises;
  • Engaging in any attempt at wrongful confinement of teachers, offices, employees and students of the college or camping inside
  • Breach of the provisions of the Policies, Codes, Rules and Regulations of the college;
  • An act of ragging which includes the action of teasing, playing a practical joke upon someone, display of noisy, disorderly conduct, teasing by rough or rude treatment or handling, including such activities which causes or likely to cause annoyance, undue hardship, physical or psychological harm or raising apprehension or fear in a fresher or asking the students to do any act or perform something which cause her shame or embarrassment or danger to her life;
  • Tearing of pages, defacing, burning and destroying of the books of the college library;
  • Using or committing an attempt for using unfair means in class test and home examination;
  • Disobeying teachers or college administration;
  • Neglects her academic work or other assignments and without any reason absents herself from the class;
PERSONS AUTHORIZED TO TAKE DISCIPLINARY ACTION
The following persons are authorized to take disciplinary action by way of imposing penalties as specified under the heading ‘Range of Penalties’.
    1. The Chief Proctor
    2. Disciplinary Committee
    3. Principal of the College
Any penalty enumerated under the heading ‘Range of Penalties’ may be imposed by the Principal of the college upon the recommendation of a Teacher, Head of the Department, Librarian, college Controller of Examination, Incharge Sports, Head of any College Society, Discipline Committee.
RANGE OF PENALTIES
The following penalties may, for an act of indiscipline or misconduct, or for sufficient reasons, be imposed on a student, namely:
  1. Written warning and information to the guardian
  2. Fine from student
  3. Suspension or cancellation of scholarships, fellowship or any financial assistance from any source or recommendation to that effect to the sanctioning agency
  4. Recovery of pecuniary loss caused to college Property
  5. Disqualifying from holding any representative position in the Class/ College/ in similar other bodies
  6. Expulsion from the College/Department/Faculty/Library for a specified period
  7. Debarring from an examination
  8. Struck-off the college rolls for the current academic year/session or for any extended period
